=== Chameleon Team ===
A Chameleon Team is a cybersecurity concept that combines elements of Red Teaming (offense) and Blue Teaming (defense) through the use of AI-driven active adaptation. Unlike static honeypots, a Chameleon Team utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to dynamically generate deception environments that mimic specific targets based on the attacker's behavior. This methodology allows for "Active Defense" and high-fidelity attribution by engaging adversaries in prolonged, realistic interactions. The term was coined in 2026 in relation to the "RabbitHole" project.
